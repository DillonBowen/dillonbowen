# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective marketing clients, employers, and collaborators checking out Dillon Bowen after meeting him or finding Dillys Trading CO. They arrive skeptical of generic "marketing strategist" claims and stay for the unusual resume: film grip on a Disney production, concrete form setter, bartender, fundraiser organizer, founder.

## Product Purpose

A single-page personal site (dillonbowen.netlify.app) that proves the tagline "Sharp thinking, human execution" through real stories and photographs rather than claims. Success: a visitor reads at least one full story, believes the person is real and capable, and emails.

## Brand Personality

Editorial authority with human warmth (from the Dilly's Trading Co. brand guide). Serious publication meets founder-led operation. Deliberate, worked-with-hands, unhurried. The blacks and greys carry authority; cream and gold carry warmth.

## Anti-references

- Tech-startup brightness, lifestyle pastels, corporate navy (explicitly banned in the brand guide)
- Generic personal-brand templates: hero metric rows, identical card grids, stock-photo gloss
- Aphoristic LinkedIn voice; the stories carry the weight, not slogans

## Design Principles

1. **Palette hierarchy is non-negotiable**: Noir Black dominant (50–60%), Archival Cream ground (25–35%), Darkroom Grey supporting (10–15%), Aperture Gold as earned attention (3–8%). Gold is a spotlight, never wallpaper.
2. **Show the receipts**: real photographs (press stills, job sites, fundraiser) over decoration.
3. **One story per fold**: long scroll, deliberate pacing, single dominant idea per viewport.
4. **Motion as film language**: parallax depth and reveals that feel like camera moves, not UI effects.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast throughout. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative (no parallax, instant reveals). Site must be fully readable with JavaScript disabled.
